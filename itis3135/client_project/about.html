About Deliverr Trucking LLC

Deliverr Trucking LLC is a small, owner-operated trucking company founded by Juan Contreras and based in Charlotte, North Carolina.

We started Deliverr Trucking with a simple idea: deliver what people need, when they need it, with care. From local moves for families to final mile deliveries for national builders, we bring big-company professionalism with small-business attention to detail.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be the trucking partner customers can rely on: responsive, transparent, and respectful of both schedules and homes. Every delivery is treated as if it were our own property on board.

  • Safety first: secure loading, smart routing, and careful driving.
  • Clear communication: we confirm instructions and keep you updated.
  • Professional on site: clean trucks, courteous crews, and tidy work areas.

Where We Operate

We are based in Charlotte, NC and commonly serve:

  • Greater Charlotte metro area
  • Regional routes across the Carolinas and the Southeast
  • Long-distance hot shot loads by request

Have a run that is outside this area? Reach out on our Estimate page and we’ll let you know if we can help or refer you to a trusted partner.

Detailed Services

Moving Services

For local moves, we provide a 26 ft box truck and a small crew. Our standard package includes 2 movers and the truck starting at $75/hour. This works well for apartments, small homes, and office moves.

Final Mile Delivery & Installation

We work with builders, contractors, and retailers to deliver and install products in both new construction and existing homes. Appliances, fixtures, and other goods are placed where they belong, with care for floors, walls, and customer expectations.

Freight Hauling & Hot Shot

For freight that needs to go now, we offer hot shot and box-truck hauling. Pricing is typically mileage-based for loads over about 175 miles.

  • Typical freight rates: about $2.25–$2.75 per mile, lane-dependent.
  • Full truckload options for general freight and equipment.
  • Load and go, or no-touch freight if you prefer to handle loading.

Driver-for-Hire

If you own the truck but need a CDL-qualified driver, we can help. Our drivers can operate your vehicle and move your freight safely.

Driver pay starts at $35/hour for local work, or around $1.00 per mile for longer distances.

Safety & Compliance

  • Cargo capacity up to 8,000 lbs (solo driver) or 7,500 lbs (team).
  • Freight must not exceed 96" tall or 93" wide.
  • We do not hold hazmat endorsements, so we cannot transport hazardous materials.
  • We require a stated cash value of the cargo for insurance and compliance before every trip.

Questions about a specific load? Send us the details and we’ll confirm if it fits our equipment and capacity.

Scheduling & Lead Times

Standard scheduling is handled by phone or email between 6:00 AM and 4:00 PM.

  • Deliveries should be scheduled about two weeks in advance whenever possible.
  • This allows us to plan routes, prepare equipment, and ensure on-time service.
  • Emergency or ASAP loads may be available depending on the schedule.

For urgent shipments, mention that it is an emergency in your message and we will respond with options as quickly as possible.
