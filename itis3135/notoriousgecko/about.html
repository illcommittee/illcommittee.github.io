About Notorious Gecko

Notorious Gecko is a micro-brand that makes playful, limited-batch accessories for phones and small gadgets. Think: textured cases with gecko-inspired grip, bright cable tags so cords stop “walking away,” and tiny clip-ons that keep chargers tidy in your bag.

Every item is designed to solve a small annoyance with a little personality. If it makes your device easier to carry, hold, or find, we consider it a win.
