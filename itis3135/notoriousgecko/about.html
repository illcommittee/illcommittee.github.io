About Notorious Gecko

The Notorious Gecko began as a running joke about a tiny lizard with legendary grip. That “stick-with-it” energy became our design compass: make accessories that actually help people hang on - to their phones, their cables, and their sanity.

We keep the catalog small on purpose: grippy cases, cable clips, stand-in-your-pocket phone stands, and bags that don’t shed zippers under stress. If it doesn’t improve everyday tech life, it doesn’t make the cut.
